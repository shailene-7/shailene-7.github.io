My Journey

I began my career as a journalist at The Paper, where I learned the power of words to uncover truth and spark conversation. Covering breaking news and deep investigative stories, I developed a sharp eye for detail and a deep respect for accuracy and integrity. Those years taught me not only how to write under pressure, but also how to listen—to people, to context, and to the world in change.

From there, my path led me into brand communications with Meione, one of China's fastest-growing e-commerce innovators. I shifted from reporting the news to shaping stories, creating campaigns that reached millions across digital platforms. Whether managing influencer partnerships, launching new products, or leading my team through high-stakes campaigns, I learned how strategy and creativity can come together to inspire action and trust.

Along the way, I also found meaning in work that connects people and communities, whether through organizing charity events for children with autism, supporting local coffee culture, or volunteering at shelters and cultural institutions. These experiences remind me that communications is not just about content, but about people: building bridges, amplifying voices, and creating moments that matter.
